About

Hello! I'm Clayton Blough, a passionate game designer and gameplay programmer based in California, USA.

My passion for video games pushed me to start teaching myself game development over a decade ago. I now have half a decade of experience working on indie MMORPG titles. I love being able to design and develop interactive experiences that people can enjoy together. I take a lot of joy in coming up with ideas for new adventurous areas, formidable enemies, or inventive weapons and bringing them to life.
